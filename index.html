Village Tsogsty


Welcome to this beautiful homestay at village Tsogsty. The village has 4 homes and all around the village you can see apple, apricot and walnut farms. A water spring flows just beside the village, you can take a hike to it's source. You can stroll in the farms and enjoy the view of mountains and the mighty Zanskar river that has carved the valley.
Help out the homestay owner in their farms, day activities and kitchen. Try archery and see ancient copper art. Buy some artifacts and farm produce to support village families.



Tsogsti gives an immersive experience of Ladakh's ancient culture, scenery & culinary flavors, and you'll create memories for life.

Solar electrification of Tsogsty by Mountain Homestays

Till Sept 2016, the village remained in darkness. The only source of light was the unhealthy and polluting kerosene lamps. There was no electricity and hence not enough reasons for the tourists to stay here. Situated at the Zanskar valley of Ladakh, the village has remained ignored for a long time.

We surveyed this village in early 2016 and few months later our team reached here to deliver the promise of sustainable and clean solar electricity.


Better livelihood through tourism

We have taken our partnership with villagers a step further. Mountain Homestays have setup quality rooms with all essential amenities in this remote village.



Apart from the stay, you get to experience an immersion into local culture and way of living. Do not miss to try the local food and see the beautiful waterfall. Also, take a hike to nearby valley, drink water from the spring and walk through the farms. Help the women in their day to day activities. Learn a local cuisine; all the local food that you get here is completely organic and fresh from farms.
